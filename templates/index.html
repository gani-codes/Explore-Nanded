Why should you visit Nanded ?

Well, there are alot of reasons to visit Nanded. It's culture, scenic views, natural beauty and of course the river bank of Godavari. Some of these reasons you can see in the photos below. You'd love to visit this city after seeing them.

Food at Nanded

Nanded is also well known for its food. Its specifically famous for its street food. It also have some standard quality hotels and restaurants. It also has various cafes that attract millenials.

Scenic Beauty

Nanded is rich with its natural scenery. It is surrounder by Godavari and Ashna rivers. It also has some hills with lush green plants. Sunset at river banks of Godavari is something which every Nandedkar loves.

Nanded is also blessed with some soothing and relaxing roads which are very good for a walk around city.
